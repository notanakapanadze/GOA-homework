Welcome to the World of Georgian Cinema

Georgian cinema has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century. It reflects the unique cultural, political, and social atmosphere of Georgia through various decades.

Prominent Films

  • Pirosmani - A masterpiece about the famous Georgian painter Niko Pirosmani.

  • The Sun of the Sleepless - A film that reflects the Georgian psyche post-Soviet Union.

Explore the Beauty of Traditional Georgian Songs

Georgia is known for its polyphonic singing style, which is recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage. These songs are passed down through generations and are an important part of Georgian culture.

Famous Georgian Songs

  • Chakrulo - A war song that has become a symbol of Georgian musical tradition.

  • Tsintskaro - A hauntingly beautiful folk song often performed a cappella.

Feel the rhythm of Georgia through its music!

Discover the Traditions of Georgia

Georgians hold many unique traditions that have been passed down for centuries. From the art of toasting with wine during "Supra" to the hospitality shown to guests, Georgian culture is steeped in history.

Key Traditions

  • Supra - A traditional feast with speeches, toasts, and lots of food and wine.

  • Tamada - The toastmaster at the Supra, responsible for guiding the feast.

Learn more about Georgian traditions and the essence of Georgian hospitality!

Welcome to Tbilisi

Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, is a beautiful blend of the old and new. With its winding alleys, sulfur baths, and modern architecture, it captures the heart of every visitor.

What to Explore

  • Narikala Fortress - A historical monument that offers a stunning view of the city.

  • Rustaveli Avenue - The bustling main street lined with cafes, shops, and theaters.

Discover the soul of Georgia through its capital, Tbilisi!
