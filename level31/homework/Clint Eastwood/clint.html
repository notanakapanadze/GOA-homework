About Clint Eastwood

Clint Eastwood is an iconic figure in American cinema. Known for his roles in Westerns and action films, he has also earned acclaim as a director and producer.

Career Highlights

  • The Good, the Bad and the Ugly (1966)
  • Dirty Harry (1971)
  • Unforgiven (1992)
  • Million Dollar Baby (2004)

Awards

Clint Eastwood has won numerous awards, including Academy Awards for Best Director and Best Picture for Unforgiven and Million Dollar Baby.
