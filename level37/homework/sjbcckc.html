Types of Sea Creatures

Fish

Fish are aquatic animals that have gills and fins.

Sea Mammals

These include whales, dolphins, and seals, which are warm-blooded.

Crustaceans

Crustaceans have a hard exoskeleton and include crabs and lobsters.
